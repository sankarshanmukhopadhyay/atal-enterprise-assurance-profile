# Reconstruction Drills (EAP-TEST-2)

## Objective
Prove incident narratives can be reconstructed from exported evidence without relying on proprietary vendor tooling.

## Minimum frequency
- L1: annually (recommended)
- L2/L3: at least every 6 months (mandatory)

## L3 additional requirement
At least one drill must assume vendor dashboards/tooling are unavailable.
